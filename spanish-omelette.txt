Recipe for 4 persons

Ingredients:
- 6 eggs
- 3-4 potatoes
- 1 onion
- 1 green pepper
- olive oil
- salt
- parsley

Instructions:
->	Chop the onion
->	Scrape potatoes and cut into thick slices (or small dices
	alternatively)
->	Heat the oil and add potatoes and onion. Stew for 20-30 minutes
->	Strain potatoes and onions through a colander into a bowl
->	Beat the eggs and stir into the potatoes. Add the parsley and salt
	and pepper.
->	Heat a little of oil in an another pan. When heated, put everything
	into the pan and cook both sides of the omelette on a moderate heat.
->	Ready!
